What Changed
v0.7.0 Changelog

Stability and refined combat logic.

01

Velocity & Network Fix

Implemented hardened network hooks for the Velocity module. Resolved a critical bug that prevented knockback reduction and improved overall packet stability.

02

Logger 2.0 (Stealth)

Introduced a silent-by-default logger. Toggles for console and file logging are now available in the HUD settings for maximum screenshare safety.

03

Module Audit cleanup

Cleaned up dead settings in SpeedBridge and exposed advanced AntiBot thresholds in the ClickGUI. Expanded combat weapon detection.

04

Rendering & Stability

Refined billboarded health bars and added extensive null-safety checks to prevent connection timeouts on high-lag servers.
