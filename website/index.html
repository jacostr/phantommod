What Changed
v1.0.6 Changelog

Sharpening the things you touch most.

01

Health Module Refactor

Replaced Nametags with a scalable billboarded Health bar system. Precise width control via settings.

02

Optimized ESP

Stripped back ESP to high-performance entity hitboxes to reduce rendering overhead.

03

Better Bar Control

Introduced the Bar Scale slider, allowing the physical thickness of health bars to be adjusted in 3D space.

04

Triggerbot Targets

New targeting filters for Triggerbot allowing Players, Mobs, or Both as valid attack targets.
